Hang Gliders

Here at Harry and Harriet's, we offer the best quality hang gliders available. We have experts you can meet with in store to discuss which hang glider is right for you. For online shoppers, here are some factors that will affect which hang glider you will want to buy:
  • Experience:
    • Users with less experience tend to prefer gliders with more bar pressure and truncated tips because they are easier to control.
  • Weight:
    • Your weight and strength heavily affect both your ability to control the hang glider as well as the speed at which you travel.
  • Flying Location:
    • Depending on your location and goals, you may wish to have a stable glider or a glider that can maneuver exceptionally well.
For more information, visit Choosing a Glider, or come into the store to speak with a professional.
